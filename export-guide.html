How to Export Your Client List

This is the only technical step you need to do. Follow the guide for your platform below — it takes about 60 seconds. Once you've downloaded the file, just reply to your welcome email with it attached and we'll handle everything else.

📅 Booksy 📅 Fresha 📅 Treatwell 📅 Square 📅 Vagaro 📋 Spreadsheet
📅
Booksy
booksy.com · Booksy for Business app

You can export from either the mobile app or the web dashboard — both give the same file.

From the mobile app
1
Open the Booksy for Business app
Log in with your business account. Make sure you're on the business version of the app, not the customer app.
2
Tap the Clients tab
Tap Clients in the bottom navigation bar. You'll see your full client list.
3
Tap the Export icon
In the top-right corner of the Clients screen, tap the export icon (an arrow pointing upward, or three dots → Export).
4
Choose "Export to CSV" and confirm
Tap Export to CSV. Booksy will prepare the file and either download it to your phone or ask where to save it.
5
Email the file to us
Find the CSV in your Downloads folder or Files app. Attach it to your reply email. That's it.
From the web (booksy.com)
1
Log in at booksy.com
Go to booksy.com and log in with your business account. Click your business name in the top-right.
2
Go to Clients in the left sidebar
Click Clients in the left-hand navigation menu.
3
Click Export
Click the Export button above the client list (top-right area). Select CSV format if asked.
4
The file downloads automatically
It'll download to your Downloads folder. Attach it to your reply email.
✓ What Booksy includes: Client name, mobile number, email, last visit date, total visits, services booked. All the columns we need.
Note: Don't filter or delete any columns before sending — just send the raw export file and we'll handle it.
📅
Fresha
partners.fresha.com
1
Log into Fresha Partners
Go to partners.fresha.com and log in with your business credentials.
2
Click "Clients" in the left sidebar
You'll see your full client list with names, numbers, and visit history.
3
Click the Export button
In the top-right corner of the client list, click Export. A dropdown may appear — select Export clients or Download CSV.
4
Select "All clients"
Make sure you're exporting all clients, not just a filtered view. You want the full history, not just recent clients.
5
Download and send
The CSV downloads to your computer. Attach it to your reply email.
✓ What Fresha includes: Client name, phone, email, last appointment date, total visits, total spend. Excellent data — powers all four of our automations.
Common mistake: Fresha sometimes defaults to showing only "recent" clients. Make sure the export includes your full list — check the record count matches what you expect.
📅
Treatwell
connect.treatwell.co.uk
1
Log into Treatwell Connect
Go to connect.treatwell.co.uk and sign in with your business account.
2
Go to Reports
Click Reports in the left-hand navigation menu.
3
Select "Client Report"
From the reports list, choose Client Report (sometimes listed as "Customer Report").
4
Set date range to "All time"
Change the date filter to All time — this ensures you get your complete client history, not just recent appointments.
5
Click Export / Download CSV
Click the Export or Download button and select CSV format.
6
Send us the file
Attach the downloaded CSV to your reply email.
✓ What Treatwell includes: Client name, phone, email, appointment history, and visit dates.
Note: Treatwell's export may not always include a "total visits" column — that's fine, just send what you get. We can calculate it from the appointment history.
📅
Square Appointments
squareup.com/dashboard
1
Log into your Square Dashboard
Go to squareup.com/dashboard and log in.
2
Click "Customers" in the left menu
You'll see your full customer directory with visit history.
3
Click the Export button
In the top-right corner of the customer list, click Export.
4
Select "Export all customers" as CSV
Choose All customers (not a filtered group) and select CSV as the format.
5
Click Export and download the file
Square will send the CSV to your registered email address, or download it directly. Attach it to your reply.
✓ What Square includes: Customer name, phone, email, last visit date, total visits, and total spend.
📅
Vagaro
vagaro.com
1
Log into Vagaro
Go to vagaro.com and log in with your business account.
2
Go to Reports
Click Reports in the top navigation bar.
3
Open Customer Reports → Customer List
Click Customer Reports from the left panel, then select Customer List.
4
Set the date filter to "All time"
Change the date range to All time so you capture all clients, not just recent ones.
5
Click Export → CSV
Click the Export button and select CSV format. The file will download.
6
Send us the file
Attach the CSV to your reply email.
✓ What Vagaro includes: Client name, phone, email, last visit date, total visits, and services history.

Manual / Spreadsheet
Excel, Google Sheets, or a paper list

If you manage your clients in a spreadsheet or paper book, here's exactly how to format the file so we can work with it.

1
Open a new spreadsheet
Use Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app. Create a new blank sheet.
2
Set up these column headers in row 1
Type these exact headings across the top row:

First Name   Last Name   Mobile   Last Visit   Total Visits
3
Fill in your client data from row 2 down
One client per row. Mobile format: 07700 900000. Date format: DD/MM/YYYY. Total visits: just a number like 4.

Total visits is your best guess — even approximate is fine. "2-3 times" → put 2.
4
Save as CSV
Excel: File → Save As → choose "CSV (Comma delimited) (.csv)"
Google Sheets: File → Download → Comma-separated values (.csv)
5
Send us the CSV file
Attach the .csv file to your reply email. Even 20–30 clients is enough to start — you can send an updated version anytime.
Your columns should look like this:
First Name
e.g. Sarah
Last Name
e.g. Johnson
Mobile
e.g. 07700 900000
Last Visit
e.g. 15/02/2026
Total Visits
e.g. 4
* = required  ·  Total Visits is used for the new client nudge — approximate is fine
Important: Mobile numbers must be in UK format (07xxx xxxxxx or +447xxx xxxxxx). Numbers in any other format won't receive messages.
