🥊 Competitor & Objection Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for when prospects push back or mention competitors. Keep this tab open during sales calls.

"We already use Booksy / Fresha's built-in reminders"
Those only send a reminder before an existing booking — they don't reach out to clients who've gone quiet. We specifically target clients who haven't rebooked in 5, 8, and 12 weeks — the ones falling off. That's where the money is. Plus we send slot-fill texts when you have cancellations, and automated review requests after every visit.
"What about Treatwell / Fresha's marketing tools?"
Treatwell and Fresha offer generic blast emails to all clients — not targeted sequences based on how long someone has been absent. They also take a commission on bookings. We integrate with their CSV export, send personalised SMS, and you keep 100% of the revenue. No commission, no per-send charges.
"I'm worried about GDPR / texting clients"
Under UK PECR, you can text existing clients about similar services without explicit opt-in — this is the "soft opt-in" rule. Every message includes STOP to unsubscribe. We handle this correctly by default. It's the same legal basis Booksy uses for their own reminders.
"Won't clients find it annoying?"
Messages are sent at sensible times, no more than once every 3–4 weeks per client, and only to those who've actually gone quiet. The tone is personal and warm — not blasted spam. Most clients forget to rebook, not that they don't want to. A friendly nudge at the right moment is welcomed, not resented.
"Is £39/month worth it?"
If you have 150 clients and recover just 2 lapsed clients per month at £40 a visit, that's £80/mo back — more than double the cost. Most salons we've modelled recover 5–10 clients in the first 30 days. The tool pays for itself in the first week.
"Can I cancel if it doesn't work?"
Yes — cancel anytime with one email, no questions asked. No contracts, no lock-in. But give it 30 days — the first results usually come in the first week once the lapsed client sequences go live.
Key context: Booksy/Fresha/Treatwell reminders only fire before existing bookings — not for lapsed clients. Their bulk marketing tools aren't targeted by absence. We fill that gap with personalised absence-triggered SMS: lapsed recovery + slot fill + review requests. £39/mo flat, no commission, setup in 24hrs.
