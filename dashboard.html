Track setup progress for each client's AI receptionist

What to collect from every new client (via welcome email reply)
🕐
Opening Hours
Mon–Sun hours + any bank holiday closures. Ask them to type it out or copy from their website.
💷
Services & Pricing
Full price list for every service. A photo of a menu board or website screenshot is fine — no need to type it all out.
📅
Booking Link
Fresha, Booksy, Calendly, or any booking page. If they don't have one the AI just takes a name and number instead.
📞
Business Phone Number
The number their customers call — this is the one that gets forwarded to the AI. Their existing number, nothing new needed.
📱
Owner Mobile
Where booking alert texts get sent. Usually their personal number. Must be able to receive standard UK texts.
📝
Special Info (optional)
Anything extra — parking info, address, common FAQs, areas they cover, anything a caller might ask about.
New Client Voice Setup
This is the number you buy in Twilio and connect to VAPI. Client forwards their calls to this number. Added to the live email automatically.

How this works
1 — NEW
Customer pays on Stripe. Click Send Welcome Email — Gmail opens pre-filled asking for their hours, pricing, and booking link. Hit send.
2 — WELCOME SENT
Waiting for their reply with hours/pricing. If no reply in 24hrs click Send Nudge. When they reply, click Mark Info Received.
3 — INFO RECEIVED
Build their AI script in VAPI (~30 mins). Tick off Voice Setup steps. When all done, click Send Live Email.
4 — LIVE
All done. Client sets up call forwarding (30 seconds). AI answers their calls 24/7. Nothing more needed unless they want updates.

🤖 Claude API Key — Website Scraping
Paste a client's website URL in their customer form and click Scrape Site — Claude reads the whole page and auto-fills their hours, pricing, services, address, FAQs, and anything else a caller might ask. Stored in your browser only — never in the code or on GitHub.
Get your key at console.anthropic.com → API Keys → Create Key  ·  ~£0.01 per scrape
💾 Backup / Export Data
Downloads all your customers, workflows, and tasks as a JSON file. Do this regularly — your data lives in this browser only.
Reset To-Do List
Resets your task list to the current defaults. Customer and revenue data is not affected.
Setup guide
  1. Go to script.google.com and click New Project
  2. Delete the default code and paste the contents of assets/apps-script.gs
  3. Click Deploy → New deployment
  4. Type: Web app — Execute as: Me — Who has access: Anyone
  5. Click Deploy, copy the URL, paste it above
ℹ️ Do you need Zapier?

No — your sales page already sends customer data to your Google Sheet automatically the moment someone clicks Get Started. The dashboard picks it up within 60 seconds.

Just check Stripe manually to confirm payment (takes 10 seconds), then send the welcome email. That's the only manual step.

Zapier is only needed if you want Stripe payment confirmation to trigger the import instead of the form submission. It requires a paid Zapier plan (webhooks aren't on the free tier) — skip it for now.
