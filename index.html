Questions

Things people usually ask.

Do my clients have to download an app or sign up for anything?+
No — they just receive a text message on their normal phone. No app, no account, no login. It works exactly like getting a text from a friend. One tap to book, one tap to leave a review.
How do you get my client list?+
You export it from Booksy, Fresha, or wherever you manage your bookings — it's a CSV download that takes about 30 seconds. You email it to us, we set everything up. After that, just send us an updated CSV whenever you want us to refresh the data (weekly or monthly is typical).
Is this legal? Do I need client consent to text them?+
Yes, fully legal — and we handle the compliance side. Under UK PECR rules, you can contact existing clients by SMS as part of a "soft opt-in" if they've previously bought from you and you give them an easy way to opt out. Every message we send includes a STOP reply option. Anyone who replies STOP is immediately removed and never contacted again.
What if a client replies to the text?+
Replies come through to us and we forward any important ones to you. If someone replies STOP, they're removed automatically. If they reply with a question, we'll let you know so you can follow up directly.
How many messages do clients get? Will it feel spammy?+
We're careful about this. No client gets more than one message per sequence, and sequences are spaced well apart. A typical client might get a lapsed message at 6 weeks, a review request after their next visit, and a slot-fill text if they fit the criteria — and only if they haven't rebooked themselves. It's designed to feel helpful, not pushy.
Does it replace Booksy or my booking tool?+
No — it works alongside it. Your clients still book through Booksy (or wherever). We just add the automated follow-up layer that Booksy doesn't have. Think of it as the retention piece that plugs into whatever you're already using.
What if my messages need changing?+
Just email phillipsautomates@gmail.com and we'll update your messages within 24 hours. Included in your plan — no extra charge.
What's included in the £19/mo — are there any hidden costs?+
No hidden costs. £19/mo covers everything — the SMS sending costs, the phone number, setup, ongoing management, and updates. We absorb the per-message fees so you don't have to think about it. One flat price, everything included.
