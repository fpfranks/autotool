========================================================
STEP 6 — SET UP A WEBHOOK (OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED)
========================================================

A webhook tells another system (like n8n or Zapier) when someone has paid/signed up, so you can automate sending the welcome email.

If you're not using automation yet, skip this step — you can manually send the welcome email from client-welcome-email.txt for now.

If you want to automate it:

1. Stripe Dashboard → Developers → Webhooks → + Add endpoint

2. Endpoint URL: your automation tool's webhook URL (e.g. your n8n or Zapier webhook)

3. Events to listen for (select these):
   - customer.subscription.created   ← fires when someone starts the free trial
   - customer.subscription.updated
   - invoice.payment_succeeded        ← fires when the first £47 is charged after the trial
   - customer.subscription.deleted    ← fires when someone cancels

4. Click "Add endpoint" → copy the Signing Secret (starts with "whsec_")

5. In your automation tool (n8n/Zapier):
   - Trigger: Stripe webhook → customer.subscription.created
   - Action: Send welcome email to new subscriber
   - Action: Add row to Google Sheet (client tracker)
   - Optional: Send yourself a WhatsApp/email notification

WHAT TO DO WHEN TRIAL CONVERTS TO PAID (invoice.payment_succeeded):
- No action needed — Stripe handles the charge automatically
- Just check your Stripe dashboard weekly to see who is now paying

========================================================
STEP 8 — ONGOING STRIPE MANAGEMENT
========================================================

CANCELLATIONS:
- If a client wants to cancel: Dashboard → Customers → find their name → Subscriptions → Cancel
- Choose "Cancel at end of billing period" to let them keep access until their paid month ends
- Or "Cancel immediately" if they want it stopped now
- You can also cancel from the customer's account directly in ManyChat by disconnecting their page

FREE TRIAL CANCELLATIONS (before they're charged):
- If someone signs up for the free trial and wants to cancel before day 30:
  Dashboard → Customers → their name → Subscriptions → Cancel
- They won't be charged anything — Stripe handles this cleanly

REFUNDS:
- Dashboard → Payments → find the payment → Refund
- The 30-day money-back guarantee applies from the first day they're actually charged (not from the trial start date — make this clear to clients if asked)
- Stripe typically returns its processing fee for refunds within 90 days

REVENUE TRACKING:
- Dashboard → Reports → Revenue — gives you MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- This is the number to watch grow as you add more clients
- Export as CSV monthly for your accounts

TRIAL CONVERSIONS:
- Dashboard → Subscriptions — filter by "Trialing" to see who is in their free month
- Filter by "Active" to see who is now paying
- Follow up with any trials that cancel before converting — ask for feedback
