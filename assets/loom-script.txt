AI LEAD REPLY BOT — LOOM DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT
Target Length: 3 Minutes | Word-for-Word Script
========================================================

BEFORE YOU RECORD — SETUP CHECKLIST
-------------------------------------
[ ] Screen is clean — close all tabs except the ones listed below
[ ] Have a test conversation ready in your AI bot (or use a staging version)
[ ] Open tabs in this order: (1) a simple business website with a contact form, (2) your n8n or bot dashboard, (3) your WhatsApp on screen (use WhatsApp Web)
[ ] Speak naturally — imperfections are fine. This should feel like a mate showing you something, not a corporate demo.
[ ] Record in 1080p minimum
[ ] Keep your face cam on — it builds trust. Small, bottom corner is fine.
[ ] Aim for 2.5–3.5 minutes total. Don't pad it.

========================================================
SECTION 1 — INTRO (0:00–0:15)
========================================================

[SCREEN: Show your face cam only, or a simple branded title card with your name/logo]

SCRIPT:
"Hey — I'm [YOUR NAME], and in the next three minutes I'm going to show you exactly how the AI Lead Reply Bot works, so you can see what it looks like in practice before you decide if it's right for your business.

No fluff — let's get straight into it."

[SCREEN: Transition to your screen share — show the bot's landing page or your desktop]

========================================================
SECTION 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:15–0:45)
========================================================

[SCREEN: Show a typical local business website — ideally a garage, salon, or restaurant with a contact form. Point your cursor to the contact form.]

SCRIPT:
"So here's the situation most local businesses are in. You've got a website, you've got a contact form, maybe you've even paid for Google Ads or a Yell listing to drive traffic to it.

Someone lands on your site at 9 o'clock on a Tuesday evening, fills in this form — asking about prices, asking about availability — and then... nothing happens until you open up the next morning.

By that point, they've already called someone else. Research shows 78% of customers go with whoever responds first. Not the best — the fastest.

[Pause and look at camera]

This is exactly the problem the AI Lead Reply Bot solves. Let me show you how."

[SCREEN: Move cursor away from the contact form, begin navigating to your bot demo]

========================================================
SECTION 3 — LIVE DEMO WALKTHROUGH (0:45–2:15)
========================================================

--- PART A: Showing an enquiry come in (0:45–1:15) ---

[SCREEN: Show the business website contact form. If possible, have a second browser window or phone ready to "send" a test enquiry.]

SCRIPT:
"So I'm going to pretend I'm a potential customer. It's 10:30pm, I need an MOT done this week and I've just found this garage's website.

I fill in the form — name, email, quick message asking about slots this week."

[SCREEN: Fill in the contact form with test details: Name "Sarah Mitchell", email "sarah.m@test.com", message: "Hi, do you have any MOT slots available this week? My car's due Friday — reg is AB21 XYZ. Thanks"]

SCRIPT:
"I hit send. Now, normally, this would just sit in someone's inbox overnight. But watch what happens."

--- PART B: Bot responds instantly (1:15–1:45) ---

[SCREEN: Switch to your bot's outgoing message dashboard or email client showing the automated reply going out. If you're using n8n, you can show the workflow execution log. Alternatively, show the customer's inbox with the reply appearing.]

SCRIPT:
"Within two seconds — literally two seconds — Sarah gets a reply. Let me show you what that looks like."

[SCREEN: Open the test email inbox or WhatsApp showing the bot's response. Read the reply out loud, or highlight key parts with your cursor:]

Example bot reply to read out:
"Hi Sarah! Thanks for getting in touch with City Auto Centre — great timing. We've got MOT slots available on Thursday at 10am and Friday at 9am this week. Which works best for you? Just let us know and we'll get you booked in. We're on the high street in Wolverhampton. See you soon! — City Auto Centre"

SCRIPT:
"See that? It's friendly, it sounds like the garage owner wrote it, it's answered her question, it's given her two options, and it's moved her closer to booking — all automatically, in the middle of the night."

--- PART C: WhatsApp notification to the owner (1:45–2:00) ---

[SCREEN: Switch to WhatsApp Web on your screen, or show your phone with the WhatsApp notification. Show the bot's notification message to the business owner.]

SCRIPT:
"Meanwhile, on the business owner's phone — they get a WhatsApp notification. Something like this:"

[SCREEN: Highlight the WhatsApp message that reads something like: "NEW LEAD — Sarah Mitchell | MOT Enquiry | Reg: AB21 XYZ | Preferred slot: Friday 9am | Email: sarah.m@test.com | RESPONDED ✅"]

SCRIPT:
"They wake up, they see the notification, they know exactly who's coming in, what they need, and when. Job done — and they didn't have to do a thing."

--- PART D: What the business owner doesn't have to do (2:00–2:15) ---

[SCREEN: Return to face cam, or show a simple slide that says "What You Don't Have to Do" with a few crossed-out items]

SCRIPT:
"And here's what the garage owner didn't have to do: they didn't have to sit up until midnight. They didn't have to check their emails at 6am. They didn't have to chase the lead.

The bot handled it. They just showed up on Friday morning and did the MOT."
