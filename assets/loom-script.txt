========================================================

--- PART A: Setting the scene (0:40–0:55) ---

[SCREEN: Show your phone or describe what you're about to do]

SCRIPT:
"I'm going to call the demo number right now. This is a real VAPI AI assistant

--- PART B: Making the call (0:55–1:35) ---

[ACTION: Dial the demo number — record the call audio clearly]
[SCREEN: Show your phone screen with the call connecting]

SCRIPT (while calling):
"So I'm dialling now..."

[AI answers — let it play out naturally. The AI should say something like:]
AI: "Hello, thanks for calling Demo Plumbing Co! How can I help you today?"
YOU: "Hi yeah — I've got a leaking tap, how much would that be to fix?"
AI: "A tap repair is £65 including parts, or £85 for a full replacement.
     Would you like to get booked in?"
YOU: "Yeah that'd be great, how soon can you do it?"
AI: "I'll take your name and number and Pete will call you back first thing to
     confirm a slot. What's your name?"
YOU: "It's Sarah Mitchell."
AI: "Perfect, and the best number to reach you on?"
YOU: "07700 900123."
AI: "Got it — Sarah Mitchell, 07700 900123. Pete will be in touch first thing.
     Is there anything else I can help with?"
YOU: "No that's brilliant, thanks."
AI: "Great — thanks for calling Demo Plumbing Co. Have a great evening!"

[SCREEN: End the call]

SCRIPT:
"There you go. Friendly, professional, got everything it needed.
Pete's asleep. That job is captured."
