FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is the AI Reply Bot? +
It's an AI-powered assistant that automatically replies to customer enquiries on your behalf — within 60 seconds, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. It responds in your tone, asks the right questions, and captures the customer's details so you can follow up. You choose which channels to cover based on your plan.
Do I need to be technical to use this? +
Not at all. We handle the entire setup for you. You just tell us about your business — what you do, where you're based, your opening hours — and we configure everything. Most businesses are live within 48 hours of signing up.
Will the replies sound like a robot? +
No — and this is what most people are surprised by. The bot is trained to sound like you and your business. It uses a warm, conversational tone and British English. Customers rarely realise they're not talking to a person, and that's the whole point.
What's the difference between the plans? +
The Starter plan (£47 setup + £27/month) covers email and website contact form replies. The Standard plan (£97 setup + £47/month) adds Facebook Messenger and Instagram DMs — ideal for most small businesses. The Full Bundle (£147 setup + £67/month) covers all six channels including WhatsApp Business and SMS text messages.
What if the bot gets something wrong? +
The bot is designed to be helpful but never over-promise. If a question is too specific or sensitive (like an exact quote for a job), it will ask the customer to call you or wait for you to get back to them. You always stay in control. You also receive a daily summary of every conversation the bot has had.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time and you won't be charged again. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee — if you're not happy for any reason in the first 30 days, we'll give you a full refund.
I'm on Instagram and Facebook — which plan do I need? +
The Standard plan at £97 setup + £47/month covers both Facebook Messenger and Instagram DMs, as well as email and website forms. That's what most salons, takeaways, and personal trainers go for — it's our most popular plan for a reason.
How do I get started? +
Click one of the "Get Started" buttons above, complete the payment, and you'll receive an email from Frankie within a few hours with a short onboarding form to fill in. We'll take it from there — no back-and-forth, no tech headaches.
