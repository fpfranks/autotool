Questions

Do I need to give you any passwords or account access?+
Never. We don't need access to any of your accounts. The AI is built on our end. You just set up conditional call forwarding on your phone — it's a 30-second setting, and we send you the exact steps. That's the only thing we ever ask you to do.
How does the call forwarding actually work?+
Conditional call forwarding is a standard feature on every UK phone. You set it up so that if you don't answer after a few rings (or your line is engaged), the call routes to your AI receptionist instead of going to voicemail. You can turn it on or off at any time. We send you the exact code to enter — takes about 30 seconds.
What if I want to answer the call myself?+
If you pick up, you get the call as normal — the AI only activates if you don't answer. You're always first. The AI is purely a backup so no call goes unanswered when you're busy. Think of it as an always-available receptionist, not a replacement for you.
How do I know when the AI has taken a booking or message?+
Every time the AI takes a caller's details, you get a text message straight away — name, number, and what they were calling about. You call them back when it suits you. Nothing gets lost or buried in a voicemail.
