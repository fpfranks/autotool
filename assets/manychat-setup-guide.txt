MANYCHAT SETUP GUIDE — PHILLIPS AUTOMATES
Per-Client Checklist | Target: 20 mins per client
========================================================

HOW MANYCHAT WORKS (the short version)
----------------------------------------
ManyChat is a platform that connects to a Facebook Page (and Instagram, and WhatsApp) and automatically replies to messages using flows you build. A "flow" is basically a conversation script — when someone says X, the bot says Y. You build these flows once, save them as templates, and then reuse and tweak them for every new client. The AI reply part comes from the bot sending smart, pre-written responses that you've personalised with each business's actual info — so it reads like a real person. ManyChat handles the delivery; you handle making it sound human.

========================================================
SECTION A — ONE-TIME SETUP (do this before your first client)
========================================================

This is the setup you only do once. Budget about 1–2 hours for this.

[ ] A1. Create your ManyChat account
    - Go to manychat.com → click "Get Started Free"
    - Sign in with your personal Facebook account (this is how ManyChat authenticates)
    - Connect your own Facebook Page temporarily for testing (you can create a dummy page if you prefer)
    - Choose the Pro plan — you need this to manage multiple clients' pages

[ ] A2. Understand ManyChat's agency/client model
    - ManyChat does NOT have a built-in agency sub-account system
    - Instead, each client connects their own Facebook Page to ManyChat (you guide them to do this)
    - You access their account by having them add you as an admin on their Facebook Page
    - Alternatively, they can create their own free ManyChat account and you log in to build the flows (ask for their login details, change later)
    - The cleanest method: client creates a free ManyChat account → they share login with you → you build everything → you hand back and they manage billing

[ ] A3. Build your master template flow
    - In ManyChat → Automation → New Flow → name it "TEMPLATE — UK Small Business"
    - Build the full flow (see Section C below for the exact flow to build)
    - Test it thoroughly on your own page
    - Once happy, you'll clone and customise this for every new client

[ ] A4. Save the template
    - ManyChat doesn't have a one-click "export template" feature
    - Instead: screenshot every step of your flow OR write down the structure
    - When setting up a new client, you'll rebuild from your notes (gets fast quickly — 10 mins once you've done it twice)

SECTION B — PER-CLIENT SETUP (numbered checklist)
========================================================

Do this for every new paying client. Target time: 20 minutes total.

────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — Set up ManyChat access for the client
(Time: 2 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────

[ ] Ask the client to create a free ManyChat account at manychat.com
    - They sign up with their Facebook account (the one that manages their Page)
    - If they struggle, send them this: "Just go to manychat.com, click 'Get Started Free', and sign in with your Facebook. Then send me your login details and I'll take it from there."
[ ] Once they've created the account, ask them to share the login with you (email + password)
[ ] Log in as them and connect their Facebook Page:
    - Go to Settings → Connected Accounts → Facebook → Connect → select their page
[ ] While there, also connect Instagram if they have it linked to their Facebook Page:
    - Settings → Connected Accounts → Instagram → Connect

────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 3 — Build the welcome message and main auto-reply flow
(Time: 8 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────

Once you're logged into their ManyChat account:

[ ] Go to Automation → Flows → New Flow
[ ] Name it: "[BUSINESS NAME] — Main Reply Flow"
[ ] Set the trigger: "All Messages" (this catches everything)

Build the flow in this order:

MESSAGE 1 — Welcome / First reply
This is what the bot sends when someone messages for the first time (or after a keyword isn't matched).

Write something like:
"Hi there! 👋 Thanks for getting in touch with [BUSINESS NAME]. We'll get back to you as soon as possible — in the meantime, is there something specific I can help with?

👉 Are you asking about: [quick reply buttons below]"

Add Quick Reply buttons:
- "Opening hours"
- "Pricing / services"
- "Book an appointment"
- "Contact details"
- "Something else"

[ ] For each button, create a follow-up message (see Step 5 for what to write in each one)
[ ] Set the default reply (if someone types something that doesn't match any keyword): send Message 1 again

────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 4 — Set up keyword triggers
(Time: 3 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────

Keyword triggers mean that if someone types a specific word, the bot responds with the right message automatically — even without using buttons.

[ ] Go to Automation → Keywords → New Keyword Rule

Set up these keyword groups (one rule each):

KEYWORD GROUP 1 — Booking
Keywords: book, booking, appointment, appt, reserve, slot, schedule
→ Trigger: Booking Reply message (see Step 5)

KEYWORD GROUP 2 — Prices / Services
Keywords: price, prices, how much, cost, costs, charge, fee, rates, services, menu
→ Trigger: Pricing Reply message

KEYWORD GROUP 3 — Opening hours
Keywords: open, opening, hours, close, closing, when, times, today, saturday, sunday, weekend
→ Trigger: Hours Reply message

KEYWORD GROUP 4 — Location / Contact
Keywords: where, address, location, number, phone, call, email, find you, directions, postcode
→ Trigger: Contact Details message

KEYWORD GROUP 5 — General greeting
Keywords: hi, hello, hey, hiya, morning, afternoon, evening, yo
→ Trigger: Welcome message (Message 1 from Step 3)

[ ] Set matching to "Contains" not "Exact" — so "prices?" and "how much does it cost" both match

────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 5 — Customise responses with the client's actual business info
(Time: 5 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────

This is where you make it feel personal. Fill in the client's real info in each reply.

OPENING HOURS REPLY — write this out in full, e.g.:
"We're open:
Monday–Friday: 9am–5pm
Saturday: 9am–3pm
Sunday: Closed

Hope to see you soon! 😊"

PRICING / SERVICES REPLY — list their actual services and prices, or a summary, e.g.:
"Here's a quick look at what we offer:
✂️ Haircut — from £15
✂️ Cut & blow-dry — from £35
💅 Colour — from £60

For a full price list or to check availability, give us a call on [NUMBER] or just ask here!"

BOOKING REPLY — e.g.:
"Great — we'd love to get you booked in! 😊 Could I grab your name and a preferred day/time? We'll check availability and confirm it with you straight away."

CONTACT DETAILS REPLY — e.g.:
"Here's how to reach us:
📞 [PHONE NUMBER]
📧 [EMAIL]
📍 [ADDRESS]
🌐 [WEBSITE if they have one]

Feel free to give us a call or pop in — we're always happy to help!"

SOMETHING ELSE / DEFAULT REPLY — e.g.:
"Thanks for your message — we want to make sure we give you the right answer, so a member of the team will get back to you as soon as possible. If it's urgent, give us a call on [NUMBER]. 👍"

[ ] Make sure every message ends with a warm sign-off and sounds like a real person
[ ] Read each message out loud — if it sounds robotic, rewrite it

────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 6 — Test it
(Time: 2 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────

[ ] On your own phone, go to the client's Facebook Page → click "Send Message"
[ ] Send these test messages one by one and check each reply comes back correctly:
    - "hi"
    - "are you open saturday?"
    - "how much is it?"
    - "can I book an appointment?"
    - "where are you?"
    - Type something random that doesn't match any keyword
[ ] Check that:
    - Replies come back within a few seconds
    - The right flow is triggered for each message
    - The business name and info are correct throughout
    - Nothing looks cut off or broken

[ ] If Instagram is connected: also test from Instagram DMs using the same messages

SECTION C — THE EXACT AI FLOW TO BUILD IN MANYCHAT
(Generic UK Small Business — copy this structure for every client)
========================================================

FLOW NAME: Main Reply Flow

TRIGGER: All Messages (catch-all)

--- NODE 1: Welcome Message ---
Send message:
"Hi there! Thanks for getting in touch with [BUSINESS NAME] 😊

We aim to get back to every message as soon as possible. In the meantime, what can I help you with?"

[Quick Reply Buttons]:
→ "Opening hours" → jump to Node 3
→ "Prices & services" → jump to Node 4
→ "Book an appointment" → jump to Node 5
→ "Where are you?" → jump to Node 6
→ "Something else" → jump to Node 7

--- NODE 2: No Match / Default ---
(Triggered when no keyword matches and no button is clicked)
Send message:
"Thanks for your message! Someone from the team will get back to you as soon as possible. If it's urgent, give us a call on [PHONE NUMBER]. 😊"

--- NODE 3: Opening Hours ---
Send message:
"Here are our opening hours:
[DAY]: [TIME]
[DAY]: [TIME]
[DAY]: Closed

Hope to see you soon! Is there anything else I can help with?"

[Quick Reply]: "Yes, something else" → jump to Node 1

--- NODE 4: Prices & Services ---
Send message:
"Here's a quick overview of what we offer:

[SERVICE 1] — [PRICE]
[SERVICE 2] — [PRICE]
[SERVICE 3] — [PRICE]

For anything specific or to get an exact quote, give us a call on [PHONE] or ask here and we'll get right back to you!"

[Quick Reply]: "Book an appointment" → jump to Node 5

--- NODE 5: Booking Request ---
Send message:
"Brilliant — we'd love to get you booked in! 😊

Could you let us know:
1. Your name
2. What you're looking to book
3. A preferred day and time

We'll check availability and confirm it with you straight away."

--- NODE 6: Contact / Location ---
Send message:
"Here's how to find us and get in touch:

📞 [PHONE NUMBER]
📧 [EMAIL ADDRESS]
📍 [FULL ADDRESS]
[🌐 WEBSITE — if they have one]

We're always happy to help — feel free to call or pop in during opening hours!"

--- NODE 7: Something Else ---
Send message:
"No problem — let us know what you need and we'll do our best to help! If we can't answer right away, someone from the team will get back to you as soon as possible."

========================================================
SECTION D — TIPS FOR MAKING IT FEEL PERSONAL
========================================================

- Use the business's actual name in the opening message every time — "Thanks for contacting Mike's Plumbing" feels completely different from "Thanks for your message."

- Match their vibe. A nail salon should feel warm, fun, emoji-friendly. A garage should feel no-nonsense and practical. Read their Facebook page to get a feel for how they usually talk.

- Use their real prices and services — not placeholders. Generic bots get ignored; specific ones convert.

- Add their specific local area: "We cover [TOWN] and surrounding areas" builds instant trust.

- If they have a booking link (Calendly, Fresha, Treatwell etc.), include it in the booking reply node — makes the flow seamless.

- Ask the client: "Is there a question you get asked all the time that I should add?" — this takes 30 seconds and makes the bot feel tailor-made.

- Don't overload the quick reply menu. 4–5 options maximum. More than that and people don't click any of them.
