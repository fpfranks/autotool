Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Phillips Automates ("we", "us", "our") is a UK-based sole trader service. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Phillips Automates is operated by Frankie Phillips, a UK sole trader. You can contact us at phillipsautomates@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect personal data in the following situations:

  • When you sign up as a client: your name, email address, mobile number, and business details (business name, phone number, address, services, opening hours).
  • When you contact us: your name and email address.
  • Via your AI receptionist: when callers contact your business through our service, we may receive caller names and phone numbers as part of the booking/message capture process. This data belongs to you as the business owner.

We do not collect payment card details. Payments are handled by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

3. How we use your data

  • To set up and operate your AI phone receptionist service.
  • To contact you about your account, send setup information, and provide customer support.
  • To send service-related emails (welcome, setup, live confirmation).
  • To process your payment via Stripe.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the basis of:

  • Contract performance — to deliver the service you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to communicate with you about your service and manage our business.

5. Who we share your data with

We use the following third-party services to operate Phillips Automates:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Privacy policy
  • Google (Sheets, Gmail, Apps Script) — for storing customer records and sending emails. Privacy policy
  • VAPI — for building and hosting your AI voice assistant. Privacy policy

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep your personal data for as long as you are a client. If you cancel, we retain your data for up to 6 months for legitimate business purposes (e.g. dispute resolution), after which it is securely deleted.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to processing or request restriction of processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email phillipsautomates@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising cookies.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data, including using HTTPS on all pages. Customer records are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to us.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated to active clients by email. The current version is always available at this URL.

11. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we handle your data, please contact us first at phillipsautomates@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
