Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Phillips Automates ("we", "us", "our") provides SMS retention and re-engagement services for UK hair salons, barbers, and beauty businesses. Our registered contact email is phillipsautomates@gmail.com.

What data we collect

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Business name, owner name, email address, and phone number
  • Business type, city, and booking platform
  • Your booking link and Google review link (if provided)
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We do not store card details.

When we set up SMS sequences for your clients, we receive a client list (CSV) you export from your booking platform. We use this solely to configure your re-engagement sequences and do not share or sell it.

How we use your data

  • To provide the SMS re-engagement service you signed up for
  • To contact you about your account or service updates
  • To send you service-related emails (welcome, setup confirmation, monthly check-ins)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your clients' data

SMS messages sent to your clients are sent on your behalf. Every message includes an unsubscribe option ("Reply STOP to opt out"). We process client contact data solely to provide the service — your client data is not used for any other purpose and is not retained beyond what is needed.

Under UK PECR, SMS messages to existing customers about similar services are permitted under the soft opt-in rule. We configure all sequences to comply with this.

Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as you are a customer. On cancellation, your data is deleted within 30 days on request.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email phillipsautomates@gmail.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Contact

For any privacy questions, email phillipsautomates@gmail.com.
